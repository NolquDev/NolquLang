# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of Nolqu pledge to make participation in
our project a welcoming experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to keeping this a friendly, constructive, and harassment-free space.

---

## Our Standards

**Behaviour we encourage:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experience levels
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing patience — not everyone is a native English speaker or experienced programmer

**Behaviour that is not acceptable:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information (email, address, etc.) without explicit permission
- Deliberately unwelcoming behaviour toward newcomers
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable
behaviour and are expected to take fair and consistent action in response to
any instances of unacceptable behaviour.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code,
wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of
Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any official communication channel — and also applies when an
individual is representing the project publicly.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project team at:

**nolquteam@gmail.com**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith
may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members
of the project leadership.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

| Severity | Example | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Correction** | Inappropriate language, minor rudeness | Private written warning, request to edit the content |
| **2 — Warning** | Repeated minor violations, unwelcoming tone | Public warning, temporary restriction on interactions |
| **3 — Temporary ban** | Harassment, sustained disruption | Temporary ban from all project spaces |
| **4 — Permanent ban** | Pattern of harassment, targeted abuse | Permanent removal from all project spaces |
